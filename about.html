About us

"Clean energy should be a choice, not a privilege"

Established in February 2023 by Charles Sebastian Satu, Taiyou Inc is the curent leading company in advocating for the affordability of clean energy to all. Not only to sell, but Taiyou Inc also advocates for the knowledge and awareness of the importance of clean energy to all people, regardless of race and wealth. Through multiple programs, Taiyou Inc has successfully sold many energy-saving items at an affordable rate, leading to an increase in commercial and private usage of Solar Energy in Malaysia.

Currently, Taiyou Inc has collaborated with the Malaysian Government to provide free portable energy generator to all B40 users in Malaysia, as well as several medium-sized solar panels and lights.




Words from our CEO


I was born in the second generation since my sisters, where I get the privilege of receiving a better lifestyle than what my siblings and parents had gone through. However, I later realized in my youth that my privileges are born from the hardwork of my parents. They are, however, not the exception to receive energy through means of blood, sweat and tears. Reading about SDG 7's goal, I wanted to change the norm of society and help others who are too poor to receive a better form of energy. I believe in my philosophy, that runs this company all together: Clean energy should be a choice, not a privilege
